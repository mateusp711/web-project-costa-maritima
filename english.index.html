VALUES

Our ethical principles reflect in the way we relate with our customers in a daily basis, in our responsibilities with the truth and in the cosntant search for results so that we deliver the best possible outcome for our customers, partners and consequently for our surroundings.

EXCELLENCE

The first pillar that sustains the Costa Marítima, a promise to quality, represented by the first of the propeller blades, our brand symbol.

ETHIC

The second pillar that sustains the Costa Marítima, ethics, ethics towards our ideals, ethics regarding our actions, our partners, our methods and especially our vision.

AGILITY

The third pillar that sustains the Costa Marítima, agility in our services and planning, seeking always a planned execution in the most efficient and optimized possible ways.

SUSTAINABILITY

The fourth and final pillar that sustains the Costa Marítima, aiming not only at present actions but also at a sustainable future. So we can build things that will last.
